Stroke Management App

Welcome to the Stroke Prediction 1.0 App

This is a specialised tool designed to assist healthcare professionals in assessing stroke risk.
By harnessing data-driven insights and advanced algorithms,
this app provides personalised risk predictions based on key patient health indicators,
enabling you to make informed and proactive decisions in patient care.
With Stroke Prediction, you gain a reliable and efficient resource, tailored to support your clinical assessments
and ultimately improve patient outcomes.

In this app, as a registered and logged-in user, you can add new patients and upload updated patient data
to the database using the "Update Dataset" button.
Please remember to name the new data file "dataset" and place it in the application's folder, inside the "data" folder.
